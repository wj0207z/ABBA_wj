ABBA Announces

Europoean & Asian Tour

STOCKHOLM, Sweden (AP) -- Swedish pop group ABBA announced today that they will be touring Europe and Asia in support of their latest album, "Voulez-Vous." The tour will be starting at Gothenburg and ending in Tokyo.

The tour, which will begin in September, will include 52 shows in 40 cities across 13 countries. ABBA will be performing their greatest hits, as well as songs from their new album.

"We are very excited to be going on tour again," said ABBA member Benny Andersson. "We've been working hard on our new album, and we can't wait to share it with our fans."

ABBA's last tour was in 1977, and they have been on hiatus since then. The group's decision to tour again has been met with great excitement by their fans.

"This is a dream come true," said ABBA fan Maria Johansson. "I've been waiting for this for years."

"We're really going all out for this tour,"said ABBA member Björn Ulvaeus."We want to give our fans the best show possible."

ABBA ANNUAL 1979

In 1979, ABBA had a remarkable year with their release of the hit album "Voulez-Vous," which showcased popular tracks like "Chiquitita" and "Does Your Mother Know." They embarked on a highly successful world tour, captivating audiences globally with their infectious energy and stage presence. Additionally, they participated in the Eurovision Song Contest, gaining further acclaim with "Gimme! Gimme! Gimme! (A Man After Midnight)." These achievements solidified ABBA's status as a beloved and influential pop group, leaving an indelible mark on the era's music scene.

GOLDEN RECORD 1976

ABBA, comprised of Agnetha Fältskog, Björn Ulvaeus, Benny Andersson, and Anni-Frid Lyngstad, achieved remarkable success. Their victory in the 1974 Eurovision Song Contest marked the beginning of their journey to global stardom. Alongside their Eurovision win, ABBA received prestigious awards including Grammis, Ivor Novello, and Billboard recognitions. Their induction into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame further acknowledged their enduring influence and contribution to pop music. ABBA's remarkable legacy continues to shine through their numerous accolades and the timeless appeal of their music.

POP ROCKY AWARD

ABBA, the legendary pop group, was honored with the esteemed Pop Rocky Award from Germany, recognizing their exceptional contributions to the world of pop music. This prestigious accolade celebrated their immense popularity and enduring impact on the industry. ABBA's music has transcended time, resonating with fans across the globe and solidifying their status as an iconic symbol of pop culture. Their catchy melodies and heartfelt lyrics continue to captivate audiences, ensuring their legacy remains alive and influential.

ABBA REUNITE

In a highly anticipated event, ABBA, the iconic pop group, reunited and embarked on recording their first new material in decades. This momentous occasion delighted fans around the world, as they eagerly awaited fresh music from the legendary quartet. The reunion and new recordings created a wave of excitement, demonstrating ABBA's enduring talent and their unwavering commitment to delivering memorable music experiences. The announcement reaffirmed their influential status and sparked renewed enthusiasm for their timeless sound and captivating performances.
